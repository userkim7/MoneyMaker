# How To Play

### Use commands for gameplay

#### Commands
+ U_
U_num->num^th utility will be selected

ex) U_2

+ W_
W_num->num^th work will be selected

ex) W_2

+ Stats

will show your stats

+ NextDay

will bring you to the next day

+ End

will make you bankrupt
